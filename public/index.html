About

About
ESC

We're more than just a cafe.

ESC was created with a simple idea: coffee should be more than just a drink.
In a world where people are constantly rushing from one task to another, we wanted to build a space where individuals can slow down, recharge, and enjoy meaningful moments.
ESC is not only a café—it is a place where conversations begin, friendships grow, ideas are shared, and memories are made.
The name ESC is inspired by the concept of "escape." Just like the Escape key on a keyboard allows you to step away from what is happening on your screen, ESC Café offers an opportunity to take a break from the demands of everyday life.
Whether you're a student studying for exams, a professional working remotely, or someone simply looking for a quiet corner to relax, ESC provides an environment where you can feel comfortable and inspired.

Our Story

The idea for ESC started from a desire to create a café experience that goes beyond serving coffee. We noticed that many cafés focused only on beverages and food, while the atmosphere often felt secondary.
We believed that the environment itself should be part of the experience.
With that vision in mind, ESC was designed to be warm, welcoming, and intentional.
Every detail—from the menu selection to the seating arrangement—was carefully considered to create a space that encourages connection, creativity, and comfort.
We wanted guests to feel like they were entering a place where they belong, whether they stay for five minutes or five hours.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a café experience that brings people together through quality beverages, excellent service, and a welcoming atmosphere.
We strive to provide more than just products—we aim to create moments that brighten someone's day.
We believe that even the smallest interactions can make a difference. A perfectly crafted cup of coffee,
a friendly smile from a barista, or a meaningful conversation shared across a table can turn an ordinary day into something memorable.

Our Vision

We envision ESC becoming a community hub where people can connect, collaborate, and find inspiration.
We want to be known as a place where everyone feels welcome, regardless of their background or purpose for visiting.
As we continue to grow, our goal is to maintain the same sense of warmth and authenticity that inspired ESC from the beginning.
We hope to create lasting relationships with our customers and become a part of their daily lives.

What Makes ESC Different

At ESC, we focus on more than serving coffee. We focus on creating experiences. Our space is designed to encourage relaxation, productivity, and connection.
Whether you're meeting friends, working on a project, reading a book, or simply enjoying a quiet moment, ESC offers an atmosphere that supports your needs.
We believe that a great café should feel like a second home—a place where people can comfortably spend time, express themselves, and enjoy life's simple pleasures.
